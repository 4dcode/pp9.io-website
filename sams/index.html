Smart Active Markers (SAMS)

Value Proposition

The Aesthetics Registration Platform is a project that connects many areas with enormous potential: Medical Aesthetics, Big-Data, IoT and Innovation. Growth and development in each of these fields is important for the quality of our lives. But joined together into a new, disruptive model they have the power to make our planet a better place to live in.

Supporting this platform means supporting sustainability because it brings together technology, pharmaceuticals, clinics, doctors and patients with common interests: to create a valued platform, support and to create a smart future.

How does it work?

The Aesthetics Registration Platform is an application that will be used to manage clinics, keep patient files and to capture Injectable treatments visually. The application contains an intelligent interactive module on which circles of the color codes appear on a face and can then be edited (moved) and saved. This module will utilize a color and character coding system.

Data per dose/injection that is logged contains: Type, Subtype, Dose amount, Instrument type, Injection depth and Technique used. Another feature in this module is the ability to easily take before and after pictures per visit/treatment so that access and comparison of doses and photos from previous visits become intuitive, quick and easy.

The platform has as highest entity a clinic. One or more doctors (assistants) are linked to a clinic. Patients are linked to a clinic and their doctor. Patients visits/treatments are linked to patients. Doses/injections are linked to visits/treatments. Basically, multiple clinics with their child objects can be managed and separated through one platform. Reporting on the data can be approached in many ways. Furthermore, the application contains a settings module where dropdown lists can be managed.

The system is best used on PCs, tablets and laptops.
